Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about volunteering with Crisis Compass.

What training do I need to volunteer?

Training requirements vary by role. Basic first aid and emergency management training are often sufficient for entry-level roles, while specialized roles may require additional certifications.

How much time do I need to commit?

Commitment varies based on availability. We offer flexible options, including short-term (weekends) and long-term (ongoing) volunteering opportunities.

Are there any costs associated with volunteering?

No, volunteering with Crisis Compass is free. We provide necessary resources and training at no cost to you.

What happens after I submit my application?

Our team will review your application and contact you within 7-10 business days to discuss next steps and potential roles.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes, some roles like Tech Support and Translators can be performed remotely. Check the role details for eligibility.
