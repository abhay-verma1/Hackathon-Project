Our Mission

Crisis Compass is a disaster management platform that connects volunteers with affected communities to provide rapid response during emergencies and ongoing support during recovery.

We believe in the power of community and organized volunteering to make a real difference when disasters strike. Our platform coordinates skilled volunteers, resources, and information to maximize impact and efficiency.
